The idea

WHAT

We'll be serving a 4-course menu based on basque traditional recipes with icelandic ingredients & inspiration.

Our mission is to rediscover and promote the connection between the Basque Country and Iceland.

WHEN

Sumendi will be open only one day during the next Restaurant Day on November 16 2013.

If the experience is good we may open upto 4 times per year but that it's still to be decided.

WHERE

We want you to feel at home eating at Sumendi and the simplest way to do it is using our own home!
